Innogr Learn

Innogr Classroom is an app that is customized to train students and farmers on best Agricultural practices, recent technologies in agriculture with emphasis on precision farming, all this using data science and analytics to achieve production of higher quality foods in a larger quantity sustainably so that the gobal target of increasing food production by 70 % will be met by 2050

Users on Innogr Classroom are purely engaged in learning new skills offered by some of the most qualified tutors that aim at equiping students with skills to maximize analysis from agricultural data such as

  • IoT skills
  • Soil management
  • Water management
  • Cropping system management
  • Fertilization
  • Planting or Sowing
  • Crop maintenance
  • pPotection management
  • Harvesting
  • Storage
Students on Innogr Classroom can also ask agricultural questions, have access to farm project management techniques and many others

The INNOGR platform has several application systems in which agrobusiness incorporates all agricultural business services according to your particular sector. In this service we provide two different platforms The produce management platform and store platform The produce management platform is mainly for the produce sellers and buys. It gives the access to the closest and most accessible Agro product sellers and consumers like retailers, small scale sellers and processors providing accurate price predictions and others additionally services The produce management provides price analysisseasonal production predictions news feed trends in agriculture chat room Store platform allows shop and store owners dealing in agriculture to create and online store An online store contains all the items sold in your actual store with accurate prices which is editor by the shop keeper and make adds to advertise the products .this online stores are accessible by anyone who joins INNOGR We don’t provide online money transactions but provide market to store owners

The Innogr farmers site gives services that cater for all the aspect of a farmer's life. making use of unutilized agriculture, the platform will analyze and visual such data to enable farmers to use it for their own good. It also has added advantages such as enabling farmers to get market, transport and suppliers of agricultural inputs and outputs, information on the newest farming techniques, newsfeed, prices of agricultural produce, ongoing agricultural project procedures and best of all a collaboration platform where farmers can get intouch with people from other departments of agriculture.
